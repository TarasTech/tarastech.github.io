The work that a CPU does generates heat, which is why your computer has a fan inside. Motherboard The motherboard is basically a circuit board of a decent size, depending on the size of the computer we’re dealing with. It acts as a nexus of sorts because it facilitates the communication of the other components in the computer. There are ports on the motherboard that face the outside of the computer, allowing you to plug in different components into your computer and also to charge it.
